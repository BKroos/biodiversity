As we work to discover more sustainable, flavorful alternatives to meat, scientists have extended their search to bacteria. Bacteria has many advantages as a potential additive to meat substitutes, as it is plentiful and easily cultivated. Bacteria are all around us, on us, and within us.

To begin this search for a bacteria to enhance meat substitutes, this research summarizes analysis of the bacterial culture of participant belly buttons. Using the below drop-down and accompanying charts, you can examine what bacteria live in yours, and others' belly buttons.
